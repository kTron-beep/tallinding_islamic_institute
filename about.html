About Our Institution

Building futures through Islamic education and academic excellence

Our History

Tallinding Islamic Institution was founded with a vision to provide quality Islamic education combined with modern academic excellence. For many years, we have been serving the Tallinding community and beyond, nurturing young minds with the teachings of Islam and preparing them for success in both religious and worldly matters.

Our institution has grown from humble beginnings to become a respected center of learning, known for its commitment to Islamic values, academic rigor, and character development. We take pride in our role as educators and mentors, guiding students on their journey to become knowledgeable, disciplined, and faithful individuals.

Throughout our history, we have maintained a strong connection with the local community, working together to ensure that Islamic education remains accessible and relevant to the needs of our students and their families.

Our Mission

Promoting Islamic morals and values in all aspects of education.
Fostering academic growth and intellectual development.
Nurturing responsible citizenship and community engagement.
Providing a safe and supportive learning environment.
Preparing students for success in both this life and the hereafter.

Our Vision

To be a leading Islamic educational institution that produces well-rounded individuals who excel academically, embody Islamic values, and contribute positively to society.

We envision a future where our graduates are recognized for their knowledge, character, and commitment to serving their communities with wisdom and compassion.

Through continuous improvement and dedication to excellence, we strive to maintain our position as a trusted institution for Islamic education in The Gambia.

Our Core Values

Discipline

We instill self-discipline and respect for rules and regulations in all our students.

Respect

We teach respect for teachers, peers, parents, and all members of the community.

Knowledge

We pursue knowledge in both Islamic and secular subjects with dedication and excellence.

Faith

We strengthen faith through Islamic teachings and daily practice of religious obligations.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, truthfulness, and moral uprightness in all our actions.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and responsibility towards our community and society.
