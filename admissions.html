Admissions

Begin your journey with us today

How to Enroll

1

Fill Out the Admission Form

Download and complete the admission application form with accurate information about the student and parents/guardians.

2

Submit Birth Certificate

Provide a certified copy of the student's birth certificate to verify age and identity.

3

Bring Passport Photo

Submit two recent passport-sized photographs of the student for official records.

4

Pay Registration Fee

Complete the registration process by paying the required registration fee at the school office.

Required Documents

  • Completed admission application form
  • Original birth certificate (with certified copy)
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Previous school records (if transferring)
  • Medical records and vaccination certificate
  • Parent/Guardian identification documents
  • Proof of residence
