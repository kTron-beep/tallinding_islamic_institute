About Our Institute

Welcome to Tallinding Islamic Institute

Tallinding Islamic Institution has been serving the community for years, providing quality Islamic education combined with academic excellence. Our institution is built on the foundation of Islamic values, promoting discipline, respect, and moral integrity among our students.

We are committed to nurturing young minds with both religious knowledge and modern education, ensuring our students grow into responsible, knowledgeable, and faithful members of society.

Our Mission & Vision

رسالتنا

Our Mission

قيادة مدرسية تربوية فعالة.
التعاون المثمر بين أعضاء هيئة التدريس.
إعداد تلميذ متميز قادر على القراءة والكتابة والتفكير والإبداع.
تخريج فئة واعية بدينها قادرة على خدمة وطنها.
التواصل مع أولياء الأمور لتنشيط دور المجتمع مع المدرسة.
Promoting Islamic morals and values in all aspects of education.
Fostering academic growth and intellectual development.
Nurturing responsible citizenship and community engagement.
Providing a safe and supportive learning environment.
Preparing students for success in this life and the hereafter.

رؤيتنا

Our Vision

تهيئة جيل مسلم قادر على مواجهة التحديات المعاصرة تحت سقف علمي مناسب استنادا على الكتاب والسنة، وواع بالطور العلمي والتكنلوجي.
Preparing a future Muslim generation capable of facing contemporary challenges under an appropriate scientific framework, based on the Qur'an and Sunnah, and aware of the scientific and technological development.
